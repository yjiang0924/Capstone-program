About us

Why we made this website?

After Coronavirus and Black Lives Matter protests, local leadership has been at the forefront of the news cycle. State governors are being tasked with making decisions meant to ensure the safety of their residents and promote equity. As a result, there have been various responses from constituents. Using sentiment analysis, we aim to gauge the opinion of the masses and rank each governor’s performance in each state. We hope to use our webpage as an informative tool for voters to consider when making decisions in their state’s next governor election.
